What is JuliaHEP

JuliaHEP is an informal organization that aims to unify effort in developing Julia projects related to high energy physics.

Where to find us?

  • Some of us are pretty active on the #hep channel of the official Julia Slack workspace.
  • For usage or language related questions, you should post to the official Julia Discourse forum with the hep tag.
  • Low-frequency communication (e.g. annoucement) also happens on Julia-HEP Google Group, the group is currently public.
  • For power users, you can find our projects on Github, most hosted under the name-sake JuliaHEP organization.
